How to Buy

To buy BlackJack Inu Token, use the integrated Poocoin app below, or if you're new to crypto, then follow the full instructions below the break.

Download & setup MetaMask or TrustWallet

Download MetaMask (a crypto wallet browser extension) or TrustWallet (an app for your phone). After that, you will have to add the Binance Smart Chain to your network-list. (Click here for a step-by-step tutorial)

Buy and send BNB to MetaMask

Buy BNB on an exchange (i.e. Binance, Kraken, Coinbase etc.). Transfer the tokens to your MetaMask wallet address. BEP-20 addresses start with a "0x"

Head over to PancakeSwap and swap for $BJI

Click here to head on over to PancakeSwap or use this address 0xc5550820a92537dc102ceB2dFDBBb1c2eB0FeCE2 to select BlackJack Inu. Set the slippage tolerance to 10% (sometimes it may be a bit more, depending on how much demand there is)

Buy BlackJack Inu and HODL

Swap BNB for BlackJack Inu. Now you need to add BlackJack Inu to your MetaMask (tutorial here) or Trust Wallet to view your $BJI. Lastly, HODL!
